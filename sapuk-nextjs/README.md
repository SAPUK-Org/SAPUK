# SAP;UK Website

This is the official website for Suicide Awareness Prevention UK (SAP;UK), a non-profit Community Interest Company (CIC) established in 2016, dedicated to suicide prevention and mental health support.

## About SAP;UK

SAP;UK provides a range of services designed to support individuals struggling with mental health challenges and suicidal thoughts. Our organization offers:

- Support services through TheSAPChat (available 6am-11pm, 365 days a year)
- Community events and projects including "I don't know anyone either"
- The Semicolon Project - an annual tattoo project raising awareness for suicide prevention
- Resources for students and vulnerable individuals
- Mental health education and awareness materials
- Volunteer opportunities to join our supportive community

## Website Features

This website includes:

- Information about our services and support options
- Details about our ongoing projects and initiatives
- Resources for mental health support
- Volunteer application information
- Donation and fundraising opportunities
- Contact information for various departments
- Downloadable media and educational materials
